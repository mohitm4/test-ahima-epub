About the Authors

Brooke Palkie, EdD, CPHIMS, RHIA, FAHIMA, is the Chair of the Health Science and Technology Department at Charter Oak State College and the Program Director of the M.S. Health Informatics and M.S. Health Care Administration Programs. She is an ICD-10-CM/PCS
AHIMA-approved trainer and has been involved with several ICD-10 educational training programs and grant-funded projects. Dr. Palkie has a teaching focus in Clinical Classifications and Data Standards, Health Informatics, Assessing Healthcare Quality, and Corporate Compliance. Prior to her higher education career, she worked in health leadership roles and as a quality and compliance director for the State of MN. She is a frequent presenter and has authored several articles, white papers, chapters and textbooks. She has also served in several capacities at the state and regional levels of the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA) and Healthcare Information and Management Systems Society (HIMSS).

Stacey Walraven, RHIA, CDIP, CCS, CPC, COC, CHTS-TR, is an associate in the Health Science & Technology department at Charter Oak State College. She has spent nearly two decades in the health information field with a heavy focus on medical coding. Ms. Walraven is an AHIMA-approved ICD-10-CM and ICD-10-PCS trainer. She also serves as a higher education peer reviewer for Quality Matters.
